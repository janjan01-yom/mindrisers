EXPRESSION

Is a combination of symbols that can designate numbers.


  • Arithmetic expression
  • Consists of numbers and arithmetic operators
  • Ex. 45 + 21 -15
  • Fractional expression
  • Consists of fractional numbers and arithmetic operators
  • Ex. 20/5 + 16/4
  • Algebraic expression
  • Consists of unknown variables, numbers and arithmetic operators
  • Ex. 2x + 3
