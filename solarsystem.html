SOLAR SYSTEM

types of planets

A. TERRESTRIAL PLANETS/ INNER PLANETS

Mercury 
  • Closest planet to the sun.
  • Named after the messenger of the Roman God.

Venus

  • Also called the 'Morning Star'
  • It's rotation is counter clockwise (Retrograde)
  • Named after the Roman Gods of Love and Beauty.

Earth

  • Only planet known with life forms.
  • Composed of 71% water.

Mars

  • Red planet due to iron oxide.
  • Has the largest volcano named (Olympus mons)
  • Named after the Roman Gods of War.


B. JOVIAN PLANETS/ OUTER PLANETS

Jupiter

  • Is the largest and most massive planet
  • Known for its red spot
  • Named after the ruler of Roman Gods

Saturn

  • Has very wide rings.
  • Named after the Roman God of Agriculture.

Uranus

  • It is color blue due to methane gas.
  • Vertical rotation.
  • Counter clockwise revolution.

Neptune

  • Has the fastest winds.
  • It is color blue due to methane gas. 
  • Has the coldest poles
  • Named after Roman God of War.
