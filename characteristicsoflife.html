CHARACTERISTICS OF LIFE

  • Homeostasis - Internal balance
  • Organization - Has level of organization from simplest to complex structures.
  • Reproduction - Process of creating new 'offspring' individual organisms are produced from 'parents'.
  • Irritability - Ability to respond to various stimuli.
  • Metabolism -  Sum of chemical activity of all functions.
  • Evolution - Change in characteristics of a population over a long period of time due to changes in the environment.
  • Growth -  Process of changing the size and form of an organism as it matures.
